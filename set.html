Set GitSearch As Your Default Search Engine

Make GitSearch your default search engine on various browsers by following the instructions below:

Google Chrome

  1. Open Chrome and click on the three dots in the top-right corner.
  2. Select "Settings" from the menu.
  3. Under "Search engine," click on "Manage search engines."
  4. Find GitSearch in the "Other search engines" section and click "Make default."

Mozilla Firefox

  1. Open Firefox and click on the three horizontal lines in the top-right corner.
  2. Select "Options" (or "Preferences" on Mac).
  3. Click on "Search" in the left sidebar.
  4. Under "Default Search Engine," choose GitSearch from the dropdown menu.

Microsoft Edge

  1. Open Edge and click on the three dots in the top-right corner.
  2. Select "Settings."
  3. Scroll down and click on "View advanced settings."
  4. Under "Search in the address bar with," choose GitSearch from the dropdown menu.

Safari

  1. Open Safari and click on "Safari" in the top menu.
  2. Select "Preferences."
  3. Go to the "Search" tab.
  4. Choose GitSearch from the "Search engine" dropdown menu.

Now you can enjoy searching with GitSearch directly from your browser!
